About Texas Website Design

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, secure hosting, SSL certificate, content management system, and 30 days of post-launch support. Every project includes unlimited revisions during the design phase.

How long does the website development process take?

Most projects are completed within 4-6 weeks, depending on the scope and complexity. Simple websites can be completed in as little as 2 weeks, while larger e-commerce sites may take 8-10 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans that include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, performance optimization, and content updates. Our support team is available Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm CST.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer transparent, fixed-fee pricing based on your project requirements. Basic business websites start at $2,500, while e-commerce solutions begin at $5,000. All prices include hosting for the first year.

Can you help with content creation and SEO?

Absolutely! We offer professional copywriting services and comprehensive SEO packages to help your website rank higher in search results. Our content team specializes in creating engaging, SEO-optimized content that converts.
